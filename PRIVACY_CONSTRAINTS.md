# VinyasGen Privacy Constraints

VinyasGen is a decision-support and visualization tool. Encroachment-related outputs are planning indicators only and must never be treated as legal evidence or enforcement action.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- Do not store property owner names, resident names, phone numbers, personal identifiers, or private addresses in encroachment records.
- Label possible encroachments only with anonymized IDs such as `Structure A`, `Structure B`, and `Structure C`.
- Show encroachment flags as potential indicators requiring field verification.
- Do not expose raw detection confidence as a legal certainty. Use the approved confidence labels: `indicative`, `planning`, or `survey`.
- Keep official boundaries, actual usable boundaries, and generated layouts as project data, not as personally identifying records.
- Every UI screen and every generated report must include the legal disclaimer from `docs/VinyasGen_v2_Manifesto.md`.
- Any future AI or CV module must follow these constraints before storing, displaying, or exporting encroachment data.

## Required Disclaimer

VinyasGen is a decision-support and visualization tool. All outputs — layouts, compliance results, cost estimates, and encroachment flags — are indicative only, based on digitized regulatory data, and do not constitute statutory approval, legal measurement, or enforcement action. All proposals must be verified with the relevant municipal authority before implementation. Encroachment flags are potential indicators requiring field verification — they do not constitute legal evidence of unauthorized construction.
